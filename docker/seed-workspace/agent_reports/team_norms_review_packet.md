## What was created

- `documents/TEAM_NORMS.md`: shared operating norms for role-bounded collaboration, evidence discipline, provenance, disagreement, and human review.
- `documents/DECISION_PROTOCOL.md`: decision classes and the process for deciding how decisions are made.
- `agent_reports/role_reproducibility_index.md`: index of role-specific reproducibility memos.
- `agent_reports/*_reproducibility_notes.md`: one memo per working-group role.

## Current governance status

These documents are draft artifacts. They can guide internal behavior conservatively, but they should not be treated as human-approved governance until the PI confirms or revises them.

## Main proposed norms

- Claims require traceable evidence or explicit assumptions.
- Roles stay within their scope and review limits.
- Routine user questions are batched through the PI Liaison.
- Disagreement and unresolved risks remain visible.
- Sensitive, public, destructive, expensive, credential-related, or external actions require explicit human approval.

## Main proposed decision rule

Before a substantive decision, the owner states the decision, why it is needed now, its decision class, available evidence, required reviewers, and where the result will be recorded.
