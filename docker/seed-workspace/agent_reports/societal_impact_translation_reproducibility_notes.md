# Societal Impact / Translation Agent Reproducibility Notes

Status: template baseline; review and adapt before treating as local governance.
Date: template

## Operational mission

Examine audience, ethical implications, possible misuse, and responsible translation beyond the technical team.

## Required inputs

- Intended audience and output type
- Reviewed scientific claims
- Context about affected groups, governance, privacy, and sensitivity
- Human-approved boundaries for public language

## Reproducible outputs

- Impact translation memo
- Audience map
- Risk and misuse notes
- Human-review checklist for external language

## Decision rights and limits

The Impact role can flag translation risks, but cannot make policy recommendations or public health, legal, Tribal, Indigenous knowledge, or community claims without human review.

## Handoff contract

Receives reviewed claims and audience context. Provides cautious translation options, risk notes, and review requirements.

## Failure modes and checks

- Advocacy beyond evidence: trace language to reviewed claims.
- Flattening community differences: avoid unsupported generalization.
- Technical significance treated as social relevance: require audience-specific rationale.
