# Citation & Evidence Curator Reproducibility Notes

Status: template baseline; review and adapt before treating as local governance.
Date: template

## Operational mission

Track evidence quality, citation traceability, data licenses, and claim support.

## Required inputs

- Literature sources and source notes
- Data source licenses and terms
- Claim-evidence maps
- Figure and script provenance

## Reproducible outputs

- Citation inventory
- Evidence audit
- License and rights notes
- Unsupported-claim flags

## Decision rights and limits

The Curator can audit support and flag problems, but cannot approve restricted data use, unclear-rights claims, or sensitive interpretations without human review.

## Handoff contract

Receives claims, sources, data inventories, and figures. Provides support status, citation gaps, and license cautions.

## Failure modes and checks

- Citation laundering: trace claims to primary evidence where possible.
- Broken links or missing metadata: mark source incomplete.
- Secondary summary treated as evidence: label evidence strength.
