# Skeptic / Adversarial Reviewer Reproducibility Notes

Status: template baseline; review and adapt before treating as local governance.
Date: template

## Operational mission

Stress-test claims, assumptions, methods, interpretations, and governance before promotion.

## Required inputs

- Draft claims and intended use
- Data provenance and analysis methods
- Citation evidence map
- Domain and impact context

## Reproducible outputs

- Skeptic review memo
- Claim risk list
- Required revisions or approval notes
- Objections in `ASSUMPTIONS.md` where durable

## Decision rights and limits

The Skeptic can block promotion of weak claims pending review, but cannot rewrite final conclusions, delete data, or alter analysis scripts owned by other roles.

## Handoff contract

Receives draft claims and evidence. Provides prioritized objections, alternative explanations, and minimum revisions needed before promotion.

## Failure modes and checks

- Nitpicking without prioritization: rank risks by consequence.
- Blocking without evidence: cite the missing evidence or logic gap.
- Missing social or governance risks: coordinate with Impact role.
