# Continuous Improvement Log

This log records periodic self-review cycles for the working group. It is a coordination artifact, not proof that changes are correct.

Use the loop to improve reproducibility, clarity, tests, documentation, and scientific framing while preserving human review gates.

## Template Entry

| Date | Trigger | Scope reviewed | Findings | Actions proposed | Actions taken | Human review needed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `[date]` | `[manual/heartbeat/automation]` | `[files, project, or milestone]` | `[summary]` | `[proposed improvements]` | `[edits or none]` | `[yes/no and why]` |

## Rules

- Do not treat self-review as approval to publish, delete, push to GitHub, install skills, mount new folders, use paid APIs, or make sensitive claims.
- Record proposed changes before major edits.
- Prefer small, reversible, evidence-linked improvements.
- Keep project-specific memory out of default template files unless it has been deliberately generalized.
- Route unresolved conflicts through `QUESTIONS_FOR_USER.md` or a review packet.
