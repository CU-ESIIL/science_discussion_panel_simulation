# Panel Constitution

`PANEL_CONSTITUTION.md` is the panel's operating constitution. The first live
panel-formation process should invite independent norm proposals in
`NORM_PROPOSALS/`, synthesize a draft, review it, and record approvals or
dissents in `DISSENTS.md`.

The constitution covers representation, respectful disagreement, evidence,
prediction versus causation, uncertainty, minority views, corrections,
experiments, human oversight, cadence, memory, pausing, repetition, no final
consensus requirement, adding topics, adding perspectives, and public summaries.
