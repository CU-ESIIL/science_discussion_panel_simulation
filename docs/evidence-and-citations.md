# Evidence And Citations

Evidence is tracked in `EVIDENCE_LEDGER.yaml`, `LITERATURE/`, round evidence
packets, and fact checks.

Research requests should include question, requester, reason, intended use,
search terms, source preferences, date, status, findings, citations, and
limitations.

Preferred sources include peer-reviewed primary literature, official datasets,
authoritative documentation, and clearly labeled preprints. Search-engine
summaries are not evidence. Panelists must not cite papers that the Evidence
Curator cannot verify.
