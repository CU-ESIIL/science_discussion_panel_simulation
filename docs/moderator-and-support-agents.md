# Moderator And Support Roles

The Scientific Panel Digital Twin uses one visible moderator avatar, one
organizer avatar, six scientific avatars, and one structured-record support
agent.

| Agent or avatar | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| Moderator avatar based on the public online persona of Cibele Amaral | Introduces topics, asks prepared and follow-up questions, keeps participation balanced, redirects unsupported claims, summarizes transitions, and closes each discussion. |
| Organizer avatar based on the public online persona of Jennifer Balch | Selects themes, proposes questions, reviews final reports, aligns work with workshop goals, schedules sessions, and identifies external experts. The organizer avatar does not participate in the scientific debate. |
| Discussion Intelligence Agent | Records every meaningful contribution as structured metadata and tracks agreement, disagreement, assumptions, evidence gaps, future research priorities, related norms, and action items. |

Scientific reasoning comes from six scientific avatars based on the public
online personas of Tanya Berger-Wolf, Lauren Gillespie, Jenna Kline, Justin
Kitzes, Katherine Siegel, and Ty Tuff. Their roles represent public scientific
expertise and perspective only, not private views, personal impersonation, or
claims that the real people made the generated statements.
