## Current result

The first model run shows near-identical absolute persistence, occupancy, and evenness across shortest-distance, dendritic, and hybrid dendritic networks. Dendritic networks have a clear corridor-length efficiency signal because they achieve similar persistence with shorter total corridor length.

## Dependency list

- Literature inventory is needed before the introduction and discussion can use formal citations.
- Domain review is needed before choosing focal taxa or interpreting corridor realism.
- Skeptic review is needed before promoting the efficiency result as anything more than a model finding.
- The PI needs to choose whether Phase 1 prioritizes absolute persistence, efficiency, robustness, or reduced competitive exclusion.

## Main risk

The current model can be used to support a cautious efficiency hypothesis, but not a strong coexistence claim.
