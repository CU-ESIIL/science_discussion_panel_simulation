# Scientific Director Reproducibility Notes

Status: draft; not yet human-approved.
Date: 2026-05-17

## Operational mission

Maintain coherence between the research question, evidence, methods, synthesis, and review gates.

## Required inputs

- `PROJECT_CHARTER.md`, `TEAM_BRIEF.md`, `ROADMAP.md`
- Current assumptions, decisions, and unresolved PI questions
- Evidence inventories, analysis plans, and role reports

## Reproducible outputs

- Project scope updates
- Phase criteria and status notes
- Decision entries in `DECISIONS.md`
- Claim-readiness recommendations

## Decision rights and limits

The Scientific Director can propose scientific scope and phase movement, but cannot approve public claims, sensitive claims, publication, data deletion, credentials, or external actions.

## Handoff contract

Receives evidence and objections from all roles. Provides project-level priorities, phase gates, and claim-readiness direction.

## Failure modes and checks

- Over-broad scope: check against charter and useful-result definition.
- Premature synthesis: require evidence and Skeptic review.
- Hidden disagreement: preserve objections in `ASSUMPTIONS.md` or `agent_reports/`.
