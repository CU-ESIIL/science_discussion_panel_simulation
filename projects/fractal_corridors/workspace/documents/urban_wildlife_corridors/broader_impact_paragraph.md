The development of scalable, streaming‑first urban wildlife corridor models provides city planners, conservation agencies, and community stakeholders with actionable tools to assess and enhance habitat connectivity in rapidly urbanizing landscapes. By integrating open‑source satellite imagery, citizen‑science species traits, and realistic road‑mortality risk layers, the workflow enables evidence‑based decisions that balance ecological benefits with social infrastructure needs. Outreach will include a concise policy brief for municipal officials, an interactive web dashboard for the public, and a series of workshop webinars targeting under‑represented urban communities to promote equitable green‑space planning.
